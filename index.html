Rules to play the Game

Step 1: Press any key to start the game.
Step 2: Any random box will blink by itself.
Step 3: Click the blinked box.
Step 4: Again any random box will blink by itself.
Step 5: Now Click on the 1st blinked box and then 2nd blinked box.
Step 6: Again any random box will blink by itself.
Step 7: Now Click on the 1st blinked box, then 2nd blinked box and then               3rd blinked box.
Step 8: Now repeat the same process.
